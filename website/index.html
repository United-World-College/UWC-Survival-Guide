Beyond Campus

There is so much to see

Changshu is your starting point. Suzhou, Shanghai, and the waterways of Jiangsu are all within reach.

Suzhou

30 min by rail

Classical gardens, canal-side walks, and some of the best noodles in the region.

Shanghai

1 hr by rail

A world city at your doorstep — for art, food, and a change of pace whenever you need it.

Kunshan Old Town

Nearby

Quiet streets, local tea houses, and a glimpse of the Jiangnan life that surrounds us.
